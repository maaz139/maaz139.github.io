Curriculum Vitae

Bio

Hi! I'm a 2nd year CSE Ph.D. student at the University of Washington. I am a part of the databases and programming languages research groups, where I work on developing tools that can automatically re-target applications written in general purpose languages to domain specific data processing frameworks. I am advised by Alvin Cheung.

I am currently working on building Casper, a compiler that can automatically translate code fragments in Java applications to Spark using Verified Lifting.


Research Interests

My research interests include compiler optimization and making data processing technology more accessible.
